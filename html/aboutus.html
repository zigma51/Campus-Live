What is CampusLive?

CampusLive is an social-netwroking site that aims to promote various events organized by engineering colleges in Mumbai area.It serves as a centralized platform for students to explore by providing them insights into different college campuses.Along with that the respective organizers get to promote themselves and also their institutions at larger extent thus bringing in opportunity for them to advertise themselves to new students seeking addmissions.

How it Works?

Various committes register to CampusLive and keep updating their profiles regarding new events organized by them. The same are displayed in the timefeed for the students.Students can 'like' it and push that event-post to the trending page

Content-Monitoring

Abusive or illegal content may be flagged by the users and will be brought to the notice of the developers. Account/s related to that post will face punishments according to the laws laid down by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology.Additionally police authority may also be informed.
